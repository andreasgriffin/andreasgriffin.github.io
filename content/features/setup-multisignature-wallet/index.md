## Guided Tutorial

The video above provides a step-by-step guide on setting up a single signature Bitcoin wallet on Bitcoin-Safe using a Trezor hardware wallet, including receiving and sending test transactions. This tutorial will guide you through setting up a secure Bitcoin-Safe wallet using a Trezor hardware wallet. Bitcoin-Safe is presented as a savings wallet suitable for families.

### **1\. Download and Install Bitcoin-Safe** {00:10}

*   Navigate to the Bitcoin-Safe download page (bitcoin-safe.org).
*   Download the software compatible with your operating system (Mac OS, Windows, or Linux).
*   The software supports multiple languages.
*   Install the downloaded software on your computer.
*   Run the installed Bitcoin-Safe application.

### **2\. Wallet Setup** {00:28}

*   **Choose Single Signature:** {00:35} Click the "Choose Single Signature" button within the Bitcoin-Safe application.
*   **Name Your Wallet:** {00:38} Enter a name for your wallet (e.g., "Trezor"). Click "OK".
*   **Hardware Signer Options:** {00:49} If you don't have a hardware wallet, you can purchase one through the provided link, which directs you to the official website of various hardware wallet providers. Click "Next."
*   **Label Your Device:** {01:01} Write your signer's name on a sticker and attach it to your hardware device. Click "Next."

### **3\. Connecting Your Trezor Hardware Wallet** {01:12}

*   **Select Trezor:** Click the "Trezor" tab from the list of hardware signers (Jade, BitBox, etc.).
*   **Enlarge Instructions (Optional):** {01:23} If the instructions are unclear, click the "Enlarge" button.
*   **Connect Your Trezor:** {01:27} Connect your Trezor hardware wallet to your computer using a USB cable.
*   **Follow On-Screen Instructions:**
    *   Click the "USB" button in the Bitcoin-Safe application. {01:36}
    *   Enter your device name.
    *   **Seed Generation/Unlock:** {01:45} If you haven't set up your Trezor before, follow the on-screen instructions to generate a seed. If you have already set up your PIN and seed, unlock your Trezor device.
    *   Follow the instructions in the pop-up window within the Bitcoin-Safe application.
*   **Next Step:** {02:08} Click "Next Step" to continue.
*   **Device Description (Optional):** {02:12} Add a description for your device in the provided field (e.g., "Trezor").
*   **Connect via USB:** {02:24} Click the "USB" button again to connect.
*   **Successful Connection:** {02:31} A green check mark will appear, and your xPub (extended public key) information will be displayed.

### **4\. Saving Your Wallet** {02:44}

*   **Save Wallet Prompt:** {02:48} After clicking "Next Step," a pop-up window will appear, prompting you to save your wallet file.
*   **Wallet Name:** Keep the suggested name (e.g., "Trezor") or choose a different name.
*   **Save Location:** Choose a secure location to save the wallet file.
*   **Password Protection:** {02:57} Create a strong password for your wallet.
*   **Confirm Password:** Enter your password twice to confirm. **Store your password securely.**
*   **Print Recovery Sheet:** {03:07} Click "Print Recovery Sheet."
    *   **Important:** Follow the instructions on the recovery sheet carefully. **Never take a picture of your seed words, save them on your phone, or type them into a computer.** Keep your seed phrase safe and secure.

### **5\. Receive Test** {03:23}

*   **Coin Categories (Optional):** Some users may have coin categories displayed on the left.
*   **QR Code Addresses:** You'll see two QR code addresses: one for coins from a KYC (Know Your Customer) exchange and one for private sources.
*   **Small Test Transaction:** {03:38} Send a small amount of Bitcoin to one of the addresses. **Do not send a large amount until all test transactions are successful.**
*   **Check if Received:** {03:51} After the transfer, click "Check if Received." It may take a few seconds for the transaction to be detected.
*   **Balance Confirmation:** {04:05} A pop-up window will appear showing the received balance. Click "OK" to proceed.

### **6\. Send Test** {04:18}

*   **Importance of Send Test:** Even after a successful receive test, a send test is crucial to ensure your wallet can properly sign and broadcast transactions.
*   **Create Transaction:** {04:38} Create a transaction sending the funds back to your own address or another address you control.
*   **Transaction Fee:** {04:43} Adjust the transaction fee using the scroll bar on the right. Choose an appropriate fee (e.g., 1 sat/vB).
*   **Create Transaction:** Click "Create Transaction."
*   **Prepare Hardware Signer:** {04:53} Unlock your Trezor device.
*   **USB Connection:** {05:00} Click "USB" in the Bitcoin-Safe application.
*   **Signing:** {05:04} You'll see the transaction details displayed on your Trezor screen. Double-check that the transaction fee and recipient address are correct.
*   **Confirm on Trezor:** Swipe up on the Trezor and hold to sign the transaction.
*   **Wallet Details:** {05:22} After signing, the wallet details, including the recipient address and amount, will be displayed in the Bitcoin-Safe application.
*   **Send:** {05:33} Click "Send" in the Bitcoin-Safe application.

### **7\. Completion and Backup** {05:37}

*   **Confirmation:** The information pop-up confirms that all send tests are done successfully.
*   **Seed Backup:** {05:48} **Store your wallet backup with our [PDF template](https://bitcoin-safe.org/en/features/pdf-export/) in a very secure location. Never share it with anyone.**
*   **Sync and Chat (Optional):** {05:54} You can enable the [Sync](https://bitcoin-safe.org/en/features/label-sync/)&[Chat](https://bitcoin-safe.org/en/features/collaboration/) feature to back up your coin categories, address labels, and transaction labels in the cloud, all encrypted securely in [nostr](https://nostr.org) relays.
